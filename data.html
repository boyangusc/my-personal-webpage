Datasets I have used

  • U.S. firms' lobbying and campaign contributions
  • U.S. federal government procurement contracts
  • Firm-level global supply chains
  • Maritime shipping transactions, including Bills of Lading
  • Shareholder proposals and mutual fund voting
  • Earnings call transcripts
  • U.S. and Chinese patents
  • IRS Form 990

Methods I have used most

Difference-in-differences

For studying institutional and policy shocks.

Propensity score matching

For improving comparison between treated and control observations.

Event study

For tracing dynamic treatment effects around focal events.

Textual analysis

For extracting structure from patents, contracts, disclosures, and transcripts.
