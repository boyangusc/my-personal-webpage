China Research Group Doctoral Symposium

I founded this annual doctoral symposium with several other doctoral students in 2021 and continued to host it in 2022. The symposium provides opportunities for Ph.D. students and post-doctoral fellows to share China-related research and build networks among young scholars.

It focuses on business-related studies while welcoming participants from economics, political science, sociology, finance, accounting, management, data science, marketing, law, and public policy. Over two years, 22 selected papers by doctoral students from 17 institutions were presented.
