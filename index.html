Management & Strategy · HKU Business School

Bo Yang

I am an Assistant Professor of Management and Strategy at the University of Hong Kong Business School. I study how firms manage flux in their institutional environments, including regulatory uncertainty, stakeholder activism, and geopolitical tensions.

Academic keywords: corporate political strategy, corporate sociopolitical activism, shareholder activism, global supply chains, geopolitical risk, political institutions and innovation.
